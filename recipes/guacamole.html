Guacamole

A very customizable avocado salad dish that you can either make it smooth or chunky.

This recipe goes well with a lot of other foods or by itself.

Ingredients

  • 3 peeled, pitted, mashed avocados
  • 1 squeezed lime
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • ½ cup of diced onions
  • 3 tablespoons of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 diced roma (plum) tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 pinch of ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Directions

  1. In a medium sized bowel, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion,
    cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour to get
    the best flavor, or serve immediately.
